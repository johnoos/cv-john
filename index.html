Profile

Academic, instructional and technical writer and editor with extensive experience producing theses, courses and technical publications. Skilled in simplifying complex ideas, designing structured long-form content, and editing for clarity. Active as a university music lecturer, researcher, private teacher and church music director.

Research focuses: jazz-inflected organ aesthetics, South African jazz idioms and practices, French Romantic organ repertoire, and Gregorian chant.
